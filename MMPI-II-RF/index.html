Introduction

Welcome to the MMPI-II-RF Personality Assessment application. This tool provides a comprehensive evaluation of personality and psychopathology based on the Minnesota Multiphasic Personality Inventory-2 Restructured Form.

About the Assessment

The MMPI-2-RF consists of 338 true/false questions and measures various aspects of personality and psychological functioning across multiple scales:

  • Validity Scales: Assess response patterns and test-taking attitudes
  • Higher-Order (H-O) Scales: Measure broad psychological domains
  • Restructured Clinical (RC) Scales: Assess major clinical constructs
  • Specific Problem (SP) Scales: Measure narrower clinical concerns
  • Interest Scales: Assess vocational interests

Instructions

Please read each statement and decide whether it is true or false as applied to you. This assessment will take approximately 60-90 minutes to complete.

  • Answer all questions honestly
  • There are no right or wrong answers
  • Your responses will be saved locally on your device
  • You can pause and resume the assessment at any time
  • After completion, you'll receive a detailed report of your results
